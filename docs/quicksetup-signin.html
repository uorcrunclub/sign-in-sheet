UORC

Sign-In App Mobile Setup

Add the UORC Sign-In app to your phone or tablet home screen for quick access during check-in.

Quick Setup

This takes about 30 seconds.

Use this link to access the sign-in app: UORC Sign-In App

iPhone and iPad Instructions

Safari is required. Do not use Chrome on iPhone or iPad for this setup.

  1. Open the link above in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with the upward arrow at the bottom of the screen).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Rename it to UORC Sign-In.
  5. Tap Add.

You will now have an app icon on your home screen.

It will use the official UORC icon.

It should open like a normal app with minimal browser chrome.

Android Instructions

  1. Open the link above in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top-right corner.
  3. Tap Add to Home screen.
  4. Confirm the name as UORC Sign-In.
  5. Tap Add.

The app icon will appear on your home screen.

It will use the UORC icon automatically.

It should open full-screen like an app.

First-Time Use

The first time you open the app, you may see a Google warning screen.

If you see Google hasn’t verified this app, do this:

  1. Tap Advanced.
  2. Tap Go to UORC Sign-In App.
  3. Tap Allow.

This only needs to be done once.
